    The finest in breakfast treats and coffees since 1971

Donut Delights has been serving C-U with the highest quality donuts and coffees since 1971. Nestled in downtown Urbana, Donut Delights has become a haven for "Gotta have it" breakfast treats. our donunts are baked fresh twice daily and our coffee's brewed every fifteen minutes. If you have never been to C-U's hidden gem then you are missing out.
